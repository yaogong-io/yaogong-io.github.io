Research

My research focuses on generative AI for embodied intelligence. I aim to establish unified multimodal representations by effectively integrating vision, language, and physical dynamics. A key emphasis of my work is developing physically consistent world models with long-horizon reasoning and multi-perspective inference, enabling agents to handle complex temporal tasks and robustly perceive 3D structures. Ultimately, my goal is to advance embodied agents toward human-level adaptability, enabling them to learn, generalize, and interact with the physical world more effectively. * indicates equal contribution, † indicates equal advising

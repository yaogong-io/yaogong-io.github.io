Research

My research focuses on Generative AI and its integration with Embodied Intelligence. My work establishes unified multimodal representations by building physically consistent world models that integrate vision, language, and dynamics for long-horizon reasoning, multi-perspective inference, and robust 3D perception. Ultimately, I strive to advance embodied agents toward human-level adaptability, enabling them to learn, generalize, and interact with the physical world more effectively.
